Why Fansly deserves its own playbook.

Fansly is not "the other one." It's a structurally different platform, with monetization mechanics that reward a different content cadence, a different price ladder, and a different chat strategy.

Tiers. Fansly supports multiple paid tiers on one creator account — typically a $4.99 entry tier (followers), a $9.99 standard subscription, and a $24.99+ premium tier with exclusive content. The same creator can have 30,000 paying entry-tier followers and 800 premium subs and the revenue math works differently than OnlyFans.

Content tagging. Fansly tags content (kink, aesthetic, scene type) and surfaces it to fans who follow those tags. This is the closest thing the paywalled-platform world has to a recommendation engine, and it changes how content should be edited and labeled at upload time.

Stream + tipping. Fansly's live Stream feature, paired with coin-denominated tipping, drives concentrated spend windows. A two-hour Stream can routinely outperform a week of PPV from the same creator if scripted properly.

The result is that running Fansly the same way as OnlyFans leaves money on the table — sometimes a third of what's possible. We see this on every creator we onboard who had an unmanaged Fansly before signing.

Conversely, agencies that try to specialize in Fansly alone miss the bigger picture: OnlyFans is still the bulk of most creators' revenue, and platform-only specialization concentrates risk. The right answer is a deliberate multi-platform stack — OnlyFans + Fansly + Loyalfans + a stage-name landing page — with a single content engine feeding all of them and a single chat team running all of them.

This is what we mean by a "Fansly management agency": we run Fansly at its full potential within a coordinated multi-platform strategy. Not a side project. Not a copy-paste of your OnlyFans. See our OnlyFans agency overview →

Fansly questions.

What is a Fansly management agency?

A Fansly management agency runs the same operations as an OnlyFans agency, tuned for Fansly's specific monetization model — multi-tier subscriptions, follower tiers, content tagging, Stream tipping and the goal-system for live events. Elite MGMT runs Fansly accounts as a primary platform for diversified creators.

Why Fansly and not just OnlyFans?

Three reasons: (1) platform risk — relying on one paywalled platform is a single point of failure, (2) Fansly's tier model lets creators charge for casual followers, paid subs and premium subs simultaneously, lifting ARPU, and (3) Fansly's content tagging surfaces creators to fans more efficiently than OnlyFans search.

Do you only run Fansly accounts?

No — the strategy for almost every creator on the roster is multi-platform: OnlyFans + Fansly + Loyalfans + a custom landing page, with content cross-published on a deliberate cadence and traffic funneled across all platforms via Linktree, Beacons, or a stage-name domain we operate.

Will OnlyFans subscribers cannibalize my Fansly?

Not if the offers are tiered correctly. We design Fansly to be a more accessible entry-point (lower tier, more frequent content, freebie posts) and OnlyFans to be the premium PPV-heavy account. Customers self-select. The data over 200+ creators shows Fansly adds ~22% gross revenue without measurable OF cannibalization.

How does Fansly chat differ from OnlyFans chat?

Different platform mechanics: Fansly tips are denominated in coins, chat tipping is integrated, and Stream events drive concentrated spending windows. Our chatters are trained on both platforms — most operate one creator's full multi-platform inbox rather than splitting.

Is Fansly safer than OnlyFans?

Different risk profile, not necessarily safer. Fansly has historically been more permissive on certain content categories and more responsive to creator complaints. OnlyFans has scale and brand stability. We help creators distribute risk by being on both rather than betting on either.

What does Fansly commission look like?

Same revenue-share structure as the rest of our platforms — tiered, set per creator, lower as scale increases. Specific brackets shared on a discovery call under NDA.

How long until Fansly grows?

Faster than OnlyFans typically. Fansly's content tagging and follower-tier model surface new creators more readily. Most creators on the roster see Fansly tracking 20–40% of their OnlyFans gross within 90 days of launch.
