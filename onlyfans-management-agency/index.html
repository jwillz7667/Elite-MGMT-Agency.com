Questions we get a lot.

What does an OnlyFans management agency actually do?

A full-service OnlyFans agency handles every operational layer of a creator business: content production (photo, video, editing, posting), 24/7 inbox chat that converts subscribers into paying fans, marketing across Instagram, TikTok, Reddit and X to drive new traffic, DMCA enforcement against leaks, and the back-office work — banking, taxes, contracts, privacy planning. The goal is to remove every task that isn't on-camera creative.

How do I choose the best OnlyFans agency?

Look for five things: (1) in-house chat ops, not offshored, (2) a 90-day mutual exit clause so you're not locked in, (3) transparent revenue-share with no upfront fees, (4) verifiable case studies under NDA with real revenue receipts, and (5) full content veto written into the contract. Avoid agencies that demand exclusivity, charge sign-on fees, or refuse to name their chatters.

What commission do OnlyFans management agencies charge?

Industry standard is 30–50% of gross. Elite MGMT negotiates per creator based on track and current revenue, with the rate decreasing as revenue scales. No upfront fees, no monthly retainer, no hidden charges — we earn only when you do. Specific brackets are shared on a discovery call under NDA.

How long until I see revenue growth?

Most creators on the roster see meaningful revenue lift inside the first 30 days from chat optimization alone. Marketing-driven growth (new traffic, new subscriber acquisition) typically compounds from day 60 onward. Our average creator hits a 3–9× revenue multiple within their first 90 days.

Do you work with new OnlyFans creators or only established ones?

Both. We split the roster across newcomers and established creators. For newcomers, we look for either an existing audience (50k+ followers on a mainstream platform) or strong personal signal — articulate, ambitious, professional. We don't accept everyone — under 1% of applicants are signed.

Is Elite MGMT women-only?

Yes. The roster is women-only and our chat team is staffed exclusively by women. This is a deliberate trust and safety decision — creators report feeling significantly more comfortable knowing every person handling their content, inbox and business is a woman. More on the women-only model →

What about my privacy and personal safety?

Every creator gets stage-name infrastructure, a separate LLC, mail forwarding, locked banking, geo-blocking, and in-house DMCA enforcement that issues takedowns daily. Real-world safety planning is included — we've helped creators relocate, harden home addresses, and respond to stalkers. This isn't an add-on; it's standard.

Can I keep my content if I leave?

Always. Every contract has a 90-day mutual exit clause. If you leave, you keep all your content, your accounts, your intellectual property, and your subscriber list. We wind down operations cleanly. We've earned a 94% creator-initiated renewal rate by making leaving easy.

What platforms do you work with besides OnlyFans?

OnlyFans is the largest revenue driver for most creators on the roster, but we also operate Fansly, Loyalfans, and platform-adjacent paywalled funnels for creators who need to diversify. Marketing is multi-platform — Instagram, TikTok, X, Reddit, and increasingly Threads and Bluesky for newer creators. See our Fansly agency offering →
