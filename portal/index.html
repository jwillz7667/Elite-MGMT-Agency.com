What's in here

Your operations.

Live revenue across every platform, week-over-week deltas, top PPV performers, retention cohorts, chat shift-by-shift output, DMCA queue status, and a direct messaging channel to your producer and chat lead.

Authentication

Magic links & passkeys.

We do not use passwords. Sign in with an email magic link or a hardware/biometric passkey. Sessions are tied to a single device and revoke automatically after 14 days of inactivity.

Security

What we require.

  • Device with passcode and disk encryption
  • Browser with TLS 1.3 (Safari 15+, Chrome 100+, Firefox 100+)
  • Two-factor on the email account you sign in with
  • No screen-sharing or AnyDesk while logged in

Not a creator (yet)?

The portal is invite-only.

The portal is provisioned for signed creators only — there is no self-serve sign-up. If you'd like to apply for representation, head to the application form. We sign less than 1% of applicants.

Status

System status & incidents.

Live uptime, incident history and scheduled maintenance windows are at status.elite-mgmt-agency.com. We post incident retros publicly within five business days of resolution.
