How it works.

Introduce

Send us the creator.

Email referrals@elite-mgmt-agency.com with the creator's handle on her main platform and two sentences on why you think Elite MGMT would be a fit. Or copy her on the email — we'll handle the rest. Send it before she applies on her own to ensure attribution.

Diligence

We do our work.

We review her like any application: discovery call, content review, contract negotiation. We sign less than 1% of applicants, so a referral does not guarantee signing. If we decline, we tell you within two weeks and explain why we can't move forward.

Signing

We register your share.

If the creator signs, we register your 10% share against her account for 24 months from her signing date. The creator is notified that you have a referral arrangement with us — this is disclosed in her onboarding for transparency.

Payouts

Paid the 15th.

Every month, on the 15th, we wire your share of the prior month's net commission. ACH (US), Wise (non-US). You also get a per-creator P&L statement so you can see exactly how the fee was calculated.

Stacking

Multiple referrals are additive.

There is no cap on the number of creators you can refer. Each qualifying signing has its own 24-month earning window. If you refer five creators in 2026, you receive five concurrent monthly fees through 2028.

Year 3+

After 24 months.

After 24 months the referral fee on that particular creator ends. The creator remains on the roster (typically) and continues to earn — but the referral payment expires. You can renew with a new introduction if relevant.

Who refers most often.

Active creators on our roster.

The single largest source of high-quality referrals is creators already signed with us. They have a strong sense of what makes a creator a fit for the team, they want their friends to have a similar experience, and the tail-end income materially adds to their own.

Active-roster referrers receive their fee directly through the creator portal alongside their own revenue share. They see live month-over-month numbers, the same way as their own income.

Professional referrers.

Photographers, producers, editors, lawyers and accountants who work with creators are the second-largest source. They see emerging talent before we do and they understand the operational gaps an agency fills. We are happy to set up a master agreement for high-volume professional referrers.

If you regularly meet creators in the course of your work and want to formalize a longer-running relationship with us, email referrals@elite-mgmt-agency.com with "Master agreement" in the subject and we'll send you our standard template.

Terms in brief.

The full referral terms are sent at the point of registering a successful referral and live in our partner portal. The key points:

Attribution. You must register your introduction with us before the creator otherwise reaches us. Concurrent introductions are resolved by the earliest timestamp on our side.

Net commission. Calculated after platform fees (OnlyFans's 20%, Fansly's 18%, etc.), payment processing, and chargebacks — not gross creator revenue.

Disclosure. We disclose to the referred creator that a referral relationship exists; we do not disclose the amount or anything personal about the referrer without your written consent.

Tax forms. 1099-NEC at year-end (US, amounts > $600). Non-US: self-billed invoice on our side and a copy sent to you.

FAQ.

Who can refer a creator to Elite MGMT?

Anyone over 18 who has a real relationship with the creator and a good-faith belief the creator would benefit from joining Elite MGMT. The most common referrers are creators on our existing roster, photographers and producers we work with, lawyers and accountants who serve creators, and former agency staff. We do not accept cold scraped lists.

How much can I earn per referral?

10% of Elite MGMT's net commission earned from the referred creator, for 24 months from her signing date. Across the roster, the average referral has produced $18,000–$42,000 over its lifetime. Top referrals in 2025 produced over $90,000.

When and how are referral fees paid?

Paid monthly, on the 15th of the month following the month in which the agency received its commission. Payment is by ACH (US) or Wise (non-US). You receive a 1099 (US) or self-bill invoice (non-US) annually.

Does my referral get a worse deal because I'm earning a fee?

No. The referral fee is paid out of Elite MGMT's commission, not added on top. The creator's contract terms are exactly the same as they would be without a referral. We disclose this in the creator's signing materials.

What if my referred creator switches agencies?

Your fee continues as long as the referred creator is on Elite MGMT's roster and we are earning commission from her. If she leaves Elite MGMT, your fee stops at the end of the month of her departure. You're not penalized for natural creator turnover.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals don't qualify. If you'd like to apply for representation, the application form is on the homepage.

Do I get credit if my referral applies on her own first?

If the creator applied directly to us before you registered the referral, we attribute the introduction to her, not to you. The fairest way to get credit is to use the referral email below before your introduction reaches us by any other channel.

Is the referral program tax-advantaged in any way?

No. Referral fees are ordinary income for US tax purposes. Non-US recipients should consult their local tax advisor. We provide 1099-NEC (US, year-end) for amounts over $600.

Have someone in mind?

Make the introduction.

A short email is enough. Tell us who she is, where to find her work, and why you think we'd be a fit. We respond inside one business day and keep you informed at every step.
