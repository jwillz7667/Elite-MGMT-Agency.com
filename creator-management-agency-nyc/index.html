Why New York matters for a creator agency.

You can hire a marketing freelancer in any city. You cannot replicate, on a Zoom call, what happens when a creator walks into a studio on a Tuesday morning with a director, a photographer, a stylist, and an editor all in the same room.

Our SoHo studio is built for that. Six natural-light shoot bays. A full editing suite with two senior video editors on staff. A chat operations floor with 14 desks running US daytime coverage. A kitchen, a green room, a wardrobe room, and — pragmatically — a quiet room with a sofa for the hard conversations.

The creators on the roster who shoot here describe the same effect: same-day proofs, same-week edited masters, and 60–120 PPV-ready assets out of a single eight-hour day. The compression alone is worth the trip.

Even for creators who don't shoot in NYC, the city's presence in the operation matters. Our marketing team works the New York fashion calendar, the New York podcast circuit, and the New York editorial network. Brand work — partnerships, magazine features, mainstream PR — moves faster from a Manhattan address than from anywhere else.

On the operations side: New York labor law, New York banking, New York legal counsel. Every chatter on payroll is paid above NY State minimum, with health insurance, paid leave, and a real career track. That's the only reason chat retention is what it is — and chat retention is the single biggest driver of creator revenue stability.

If you're searching "creator management agency NYC" or "OnlyFans agency Manhattan" the question to ask isn't who has the nicest website. It's who actually has a New York office. We do. Read the broader agency overview →

Local questions.

Where is the Elite MGMT office in NYC?

11 Mercer Street, 4F, in SoHo (Manhattan). The studio occupies the full fourth floor — natural-light shoot bays, a kitchen, an editing suite, and a dedicated chat-ops floor. Mercer Street and Broome subway entrances are a 4-minute walk.

Do I have to be in New York to sign with Elite MGMT?

No. About 60% of the roster is NYC-based — they get on-site shoots at the studio. The other 40% are spread across LA, Miami, London, Toronto and Sydney. We run quarterly shoot tours to the major creator cities, plus a roster retreat each year.

Is the chat team in New York?

The chat ops floor is in our SoHo office for day-shift coverage, with a UK desk in London for European overnight and an Australia desk in Sydney for AU overnight. All in-house, all on payroll, all women, all native English.

Can I tour the office before signing?

Yes — every prospective creator who progresses past the diligence stage is invited to the SoHo studio if they're in or willing to fly to NYC. Travel is on us. Meet the partner you'd sign with, the chat lead who'd run your pod, and current creators on the roster (under NDA).

Why does location matter for a creator agency?

Three things only an in-person operation can deliver: (1) on-site shoot days with director, photographer, stylist and editor in one room — same-week turnaround; (2) physical chat ops where senior chatters mentor newer ones in real time; (3) you can walk in, sit on a sofa, and have a hard conversation in person when it matters.

What about creators outside the US?

We sign creators in UK, Canada, Australia and the EU. The legal stack, banking, and tax setup are adjusted per jurisdiction with local counsel. We've onboarded creators in 11 countries since 2020.

Is the agency licensed in New York?

Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. is a Delaware C-Corporation registered to do business in New York, with NY State sales tax registration where applicable. The parent is Viral Ventures LLC, Minnesota. Documentation is shared in diligence.
