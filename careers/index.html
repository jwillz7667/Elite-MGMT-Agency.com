Hiring 2026 · NYC · Remote eligible (US)

Work at Elite MGMT.

We're a 32-person team in SoHo running the businesses of under-50 top-tier creators. We hire carefully, pay well above industry, and treat every role on the team as a craft, not a churn line. Our chat operations and creator-facing roles are women-only by hiring policy — a deliberate trust-and-safety decision creators have told us is non-negotiable.

What we believe.

The creators we work with deserve operators as serious as they are. That means hiring people who can do the work well, paying them above the industry average so they stay long enough to get good, and writing the rules we wish other agencies had.

That means no offshored chat. No commission-only chatter contracts. Real benefits, real PTO, real career paths. And it means saying no to clients, candidates, or work that doesn't fit. We turn down about 99% of creator applications and a comparable fraction of vendor pitches. The bar for staff is the same.

Hiring principles.

Take-home, not whiteboard. We send a paid take-home that mirrors actual work and we read every submission. We do not write algorithm puzzles.

Two interviews, decision in five business days. One conversation with the hiring manager, one with the team you'd join. We aim to never leave anyone in limbo.

Salary disclosed in the first message. We publish the band on the role; the offer lands somewhere inside it based on level. No negotiation theater.

Background checks for chat and finance roles. Conducted by an accredited vendor with the candidate's written consent, per our privacy policy.

FAQ & policy.

Is Elite MGMT a women-only employer?

Creator-facing and chat operations roles are women-only by hiring policy, as the bona-fide occupational qualification for these positions (under EEOC v. Sears, Roebuck reasoning) is the gendered nature of the trust relationship with our female roster. Non-creator-facing roles — IT, legal, finance, design — are open to candidates of any gender.

This page lists open roles with that flag set per-role. Where a role is marked "women-only", we will redirect non-qualifying applicants to roles that are open to them.

Do you sponsor visas?

For senior roles ($90k+), yes. We sponsor H-1B, O-1, and TN visas where the candidate qualifies. For chat-operator roles we hire only people with the right to work in the United States; we have a small UK entity that hires UK residents for the overnight (ET) chat shifts.

How long does the process take?

From first application to offer, typically 8 to 12 business days. We respond to every application within 3 business days, send the take-home within 5, and make a decision within 5 of receiving the take-home back.

Will my application stay confidential?

Yes. Applications are visible only to the hiring manager, the team you'd join, and HR. We do not share applications with creators, with other agencies, or with anyone outside Elite MGMT. Handled per our privacy policy.

Don't see your role?

Send us a letter.

We read every speculative application that comes in with a short letter explaining what you do and what you'd want to build at Elite MGMT. If we don't have a role today but we want to hire you, we will tell you when we will.
