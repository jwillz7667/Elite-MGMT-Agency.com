DMCA Policy & Takedown.

Effective 2026-05-12 · Last updated 2026-05-12 · Version 3.0

Elite MGMT Agency respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users of this Site, and platforms that host our creators' work, to do the same. This page explains how to send a Digital Millennium Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. §512) notice if you believe content on the Site infringes your copyright, and how we enforce our creators' rights against unauthorized reproduction of their work on third-party platforms.

2. How to submit a takedown notice

To be effective under §512(c)(3), your notice must include all of the following — incomplete notices may be ignored:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on the owner's behalf.
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed (or, for multiple works at the same site, a representative list).
  3. Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing — including the URL(s) on the Site sufficient for us to locate it.
  4. Your contact information: full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
  5. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  6. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or are authorized to act on behalf of the owner.
Misrepresentations under §512(f) can subject the sender to damages, including costs and attorneys' fees. Send takedown notices only for material you actually own or are authorized to act for.

3. Counter-notification

If material you posted was removed in response to a DMCA notice and you believe the removal was in error or the material is non-infringing (for example, because it is fair use or you have a license), you may send a counter-notification to the designated agent. Per §512(g)(3), the counter-notification must include:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature.
  2. Identification of the material that has been removed and the location it appeared before removal.
  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the material was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification.
  4. Your name, address, and telephone number, and a statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal district court for the judicial district in which your address is located (or, if outside the US, any judicial district in which we may be found), and that you will accept service of process from the person who provided the original notification or an agent of that person.

On receipt of a valid counter-notification we will, within 10–14 business days, restore the removed material unless the original sender has filed an action seeking a court order to restrain the activity.

4. Repeat-infringer policy

Per §512(i), it is our policy to terminate, in appropriate circumstances, the access of users who are repeat infringers. We do not maintain user accounts on the Site (we are a marketing and informational site, not a hosting platform), so this policy primarily applies to internal accounts and to creators on our roster. A creator found to be repeatedly republishing material they do not own, or who has had three or more substantiated DMCA notices filed against work appearing under their managed accounts, will be subject to termination of representation in accordance with their management agreement.

5. Our enforcement program (for our creators)

Beyond responding to inbound notices, Elite MGMT operates an in-house takedown program for our roster. Each signed creator's content is fingerprinted on production and added to a monitored set. The program issues DMCA notices on the creator's behalf to:

  • Adult tube sites and aggregator forums (Pornhub, XVideos, ThePornDude family, MrDeepFakes, etc.) under their respective abuse policies and §512.
  • Telegram channels and groups via the Telegram DMCA policy and, where appropriate, takedown requests routed through hosting providers under §512.
  • Discord servers via Discord's Trust & Safety reporting.
  • Search engines (Google, Bing) under their respective copyright-removal procedures so leaked URLs are de-indexed from search.
  • Reddit subreddits via subreddit moderators and Reddit's copyright form.
  • X / Threads / TikTok / Instagram via each platform's copyright form.

The program is staffed in-house. We do not use offshore DMCA contractors. Creators receive a weekly report listing every notice issued and the takedown result.

Elite MGMT acts as an authorized agent for its signed creators for DMCA enforcement purposes. The authorization is granted in writing in the management agreement.

6. Trademark and other rights

For takedown requests concerning trademark, right of publicity, defamation, or revenge porn (NCII), please email legal@elite-mgmt-agency.com. We aim to respond to NCII requests within 24 hours.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our procedures or the relevant law changes. We will post the revised version with an updated "Last updated" date.
