Why women-only is not a marketing position.

The OnlyFans management industry was built by men. Most of the agencies you'll find by searching are male-owned, male-managed, and staffed by male chatters in offshore offices. The women whose names are on the contracts are usually the youngest, lowest-paid people in the room.

Elite MGMT was founded in 2019 by two women — one a former creator, one a former entertainment lawyer — who watched friends sign with agencies that traded their privacy for a Telegram fanbase and a 50% take. The brief was simple: build the agency we'd have signed with.

Six years later that brief is still the operating system. The roster is 100% women. The chat team is 100% women. The partners are 100% women. The content team — directors, photographers, stylists, editors — is 100% women. The legal counsel and the safety lead are women. The only roles with men are engineering and accounting, both of which never touch a creator's inbox, content, or personal information.

The practical effect is what creators on the roster talk about most: nobody handling your business has anything to prove about understanding it.

Your chat lead has worked an inbox before. Your content director has been in front of the camera before. Your safety lead has dealt with a stalker before. Your partner has signed a contract she later regretted before. None of this is theoretical.

This is also why our retention is what it is. The industry average creator tenure with an OnlyFans agency is around 11 months. Ours is 4.2 years. Our creator-initiated renewal rate is 94%. Both numbers are the same answer phrased two ways: when you treat the creator as the person you work for, she stays.

The safety stack is standard.

Stage-name infrastructure

Separate legal LLC, EIN, mail-forwarding address, US bank account in the entity name, payments routed through the entity. Your government identity is never on a public platform.

Locked banking & payouts

Multi-signature banking with two-factor on every transaction. Suspicious activity escalates to the partner who signed you, never an offshore ops desk.

Daily DMCA enforcement

In-house takedowns across tube sites, Telegram, Reddit, search engines. Real legal escalation when an offender persists. Last year we processed 14,200+ takedowns.

Geo-blocking & privacy planning

Block specific metros, countries, IPs, schools. Real-world planning: home-address hardening, relocation support, family communication, restraining-order coordination if needed.

Therapist referrals

A vetted, women-only network of therapists with sex-work-affirming training. You see who you choose, we cover the first six sessions, full confidentiality.

Exit readiness

A documented offboarding plan from day one: how to wind down accounts, retain content rights, handle archived material, communicate to subscribers. No surprise terms.

Things creators actually ask.

Why does a women-only OnlyFans agency matter?

Because the people who handle a creator's inbox, content and contracts shape her daily experience of the job. A women-only team means every chat reply, every shoot direction, every contract negotiation, and every late-night escalation is run by someone who shares the creator's lived context. That changes burnout rates, retention, and the quality of decisions made on a creator's behalf.

Is the entire Elite MGMT team women?

The roster is 100% women. Operations — chat, content production, marketing, brand, legal, and partners — are 100% women. Engineering and accounting (back-office only, no creator contact) include men. Anyone with creator-facing access is a woman, NDA-bound, and background-checked.

Why are most OnlyFans agencies male-run?

The category was founded by men because the early adopters were men running affiliate funnels and adult dropshipping. Most agencies you'll find online still are. Elite MGMT was founded in 2019 by two women who'd worked inside that system and wanted to build the opposite.

Will my chatters really be women?

Yes — every chatter on your inbox is a woman, hired in-house with a W-2 or formal contractor agreement, paid above industry rate, NDA-bound, and background-checked. You'll be introduced to your primary chat lead during onboarding and can request to meet the full pod handling your inbox.

How do you handle harassment and stalkers?

Every creator gets the safety stack as standard: stage-name infrastructure, separate LLC, mail forwarding, locked banking, geo-blocking against your home metro, real-time DMCA enforcement, and an escalation protocol with our legal partner for serious threats. We've helped creators relocate, harden home addresses, and obtain restraining orders.

What if I want to leave?

Every contract carries a 90-day mutual exit clause. You keep your content, your IP, your accounts, and your subscriber list. Our 94% creator-initiated renewal rate exists because leaving is easy. We'd rather you stay because you want to than because you're trapped.

Do you take new creators?

Yes — about a third of new signings each year are creators starting from zero or under 10k subscribers. The bar is signal, not size: we look for women who are articulate, ambitious, and clear on why they want to do this. We turn down most established creators we evaluate for the same reason.

How does the application process protect my identity?

First-stage applications use first name only, no government ID, no platform links you don't choose to share. Mutual NDA before any platform link, revenue figure, or sensitive detail. We never list creators publicly without explicit consent — every name and image on this site has signed off.

What's the commission?

Tiered revenue share, set per creator on signing, lower as revenue scales. No upfront fees, no monthly retainer, no hidden costs. Specific brackets shared on the discovery call under NDA.
