18 U.S.C. §2257 Compliance Statement.

Effective 2026-05-12 · Last updated 2026-05-12 · Version 2.0

This page is the federally-required statement under 18 U.S.C. §2257 and 28 C.F.R. Part 75 regarding age-verification records for any visual depictions of actual sexually explicit conduct in connection with which Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. is a "producer". It also identifies our custodian of records and the procedure for inspection of records by authorized federal officials.

1. Statement of compliance

All performers depicted in visual material in connection with which Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. is a producer were at least 18 years old at the time of original production of the depiction. Records required by 18 U.S.C. §2257 and 28 C.F.R. §75 are kept by the custodian of records identified below and are available for inspection at the times and in the manner provided by law.

3. Definitions and scope

3.1 "Producer"

Under 28 C.F.R. §75.1(c), a "producer" is a person who creates, produces, or in any other way is responsible for visual depictions of actual sexually explicit conduct, with limited carve-outs. Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. is a "secondary producer" only with respect to certain visual depictions produced by signed creators that we manage. Records-keeping obligations attach to the production and we maintain those records consistent with §75.

3.2 Scope of this statement

This statement applies only to visual depictions of actual sexually explicit conduct created on or after the effective date set forth above, in connection with which Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. acts as a producer. It does not apply to:

  • The content of the elite-mgmt-agency.com website, which contains no visual depictions of actual sexually explicit conduct. The website is informational and marketing-only.
  • Material exempt under 18 U.S.C. §2257A or 28 C.F.R. §75.7 (simulated sexually explicit conduct, etc.).
  • Visual depictions created before the effective date of the regulations that originally imposed the record-keeping obligation (i.e., July 3, 1995, as subsequently amended).
  • Material as to which Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. is neither a primary nor a secondary producer.

4. Records maintained

For each performer depicted in qualifying material, the custodian maintains, at the address identified in Section 2, the following records as required by 28 C.F.R. §75.2:

  • A copy of the performer's picture identification card (government-issued, e.g., driver's license, passport).
  • The performer's legal name, every other name ever used by the performer (including maiden, professional, and stage names), and the date of the original production of the depiction.
  • A copy of the performer's date of birth and verification that the performer was 18 years of age or older at the time of original production.
  • Cross-reference indices organized by (i) legal name, (ii) each professional/stage name, and (iii) title of work, sufficient to enable the location of records by any of those three identifiers per 28 C.F.R. §75.2(d).

Records are retained for the duration required by 28 C.F.R. §75.4 (records must be maintained as long as the depiction is in commerce, plus the period set out in the regulation). Records are stored in encrypted form at rest and access is restricted by role.

5. Material on third-party platforms

Visual depictions appearing on third-party platforms (e.g., OnlyFans, Fansly, Loyalfans) where a signed creator is the account holder are subject to those platforms' own age-verification and record-keeping procedures. Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. cooperates with platform compliance teams and provides records on request where Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. is a producer under §75. The platforms themselves are also producers within the meaning of 28 C.F.R. §75.1(c) for material hosted on their services.

6. No depictions on this website

The elite-mgmt-agency.com website contains no visual depictions of actual sexually explicit conduct as defined in 18 U.S.C. §2256(2). Visual material on the website consists of editorial photography of creators (clothed), agency staff, the New York office, and stock or licensed imagery used for design purposes. As a result, the record-keeping obligations of 28 C.F.R. §75.6 (labeling of websites) do not attach to specific pages of this site beyond the publication of this statement.

7. Records requests by authorized officials

Inspection of records by an investigator authorized to act on behalf of the Attorney General of the United States is permitted at the address in Section 2, by appointment, during the hours stated. Investigators must present credentials and identify the purpose of the inspection. Elite MGMT Agency, Inc. reserves the right (but not the obligation) to notify the affected performer and to have counsel present during inspection.

8. Updates to this statement

This statement will be updated whenever the identity, contact information, or hours of the custodian of records changes, or when applicable law or regulation is amended. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.

This statement is provided in compliance with 18 U.S.C. §2257 and 28 C.F.R. §75. It is not legal advice. If you have questions about how §2257 applies to your own production, consult qualified counsel.
